INTRODUCTION
* Select from ingredients container the desired ingredients
* With an ingredient selected, choose a tool to process the ingredient
* For now, one tool can only process one ingredient
* You must process the ingredient to make a potion successfully
* Certain ingredient(s) doesn't need to be processed with a tool
* After an ingredient is processed successfully, you may extract it
* If you're not satisfied with current ingredient, you may burn it
* You can restart from the begining using restart button
* You may hit brew anytime to generate the potion, but remember: most of potions are combined with three processed ingredients!
* Here's a recipe of [Cutification Potion] for you to test out:
[Cutification Potion] = 1[Dragon saliva] + 1[Cat attention]
